Our Services

We provide flower arrangements for weddings and other large events in the Greater Toronto Area, including Barrie.


Our service is personalized, dedicated exclusively to your event, with minute attention to detail and the customer's vision.


We are a custom event based company striving to provide our customers with individualized service to help them make their dreams come true. Whatever your special day or occasion, we can support take it to the next level and transform it from an ordinary event into something extraordinary which all your guests will surely remember.


We welcome details regarding your garments, avenue or your personal vision of the event in order to provide you with our professional opinion and guidance. Our expertise in using floral design with an European flair will provide you with an unforgettable experience. We specialize in fresh and/or silk flowers, according to our customers’ wishes and budget needs.


Our services include wedding floral decorations and accessories, bouquets for brides, bridesmaids and flower girls, boutonnieres and corsages, centerpieces, large floral arrangements for the wedding ceremony or church altar, pew decorations, floral arches, floral cake decorations and wedding accessories for rent or purchase.


We also offer customized floral arrangements for home, business, family or church events, such as anniversaries, birthday parties, corporate parties, Christmas and other holiday celebrations, or funerals. We can beautify the entry space of your business with customized urn decorations, wreaths and swags for all seasons.


We strive to provide you with a high quality product, competitive pricing and individualized designs.  We would love to hear from you, please email or call us to discuss your event details.


We are a member of the Canadian Bridal Directory.
